# Stakeholder Overview

## Purpose
This document identifies key stakeholders involved in the loan application and risk review process at Union Bank and outlines their roles and interests within the process.

## Stakeholder Groups & Roles

### Relationship Managers (RM)
- Primary point of contact for customers
- Collect loan applications and supporting documentation
- Enter application data into loan systems
- Communicate loan status to customers

### Credit Analysts
- Perform financial and credit analysis
- Evaluate borrower financial statements
- Assess creditworthiness and risk exposure

### Risk & Underwriting Team
- Review credit analysis and risk assessments
- Approve, reject, or conditionally approve loan applications
- Ensure risk appetite and policy compliance

### Compliance Team
- Validate regulatory and policy requirements
- Review documentation for adherence to banking regulations
- Escalate compliance issues when identified

### Operations Team
- Book approved loans in core banking systems
- Manage loan disbursement and documentation
- Ensure operational accuracy and completeness

### Business Leadership
- Monitor loan performance and risk metrics
- Define strategic objectives and risk tolerance
- Review KPIs and process performance

## Stakeholder Pain Points
- Limited visibility into loan application status
- Manual handoffs between teams
- Rework due to incomplete or inconsistent data
- Delays caused by email-based communication

## Stakeholder Expectations
- Faster loan turnaround time
- Improved data accuracy
- Clear ownership and accountability
- Enhanced process transparency
